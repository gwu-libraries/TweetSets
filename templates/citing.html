Citing TweetSets and datasets


Citing TweetSets

The recommended citation for TweetSets (i.e., the software) is:

Justin Littman. (2018). TweetSets. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1289426

For more guidance on citing TweetSets, see TweetSets in Zenodo.

Citing a source dataset

Datasets that have been publicly released, e.g., in a data repository, will have a public link. Refer to the public version for guidance on citing.

The public link is available in the dataset details on the dataset list page or on the source dataset tab for your dataset.

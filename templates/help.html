Step-by-step guide to TweetSets


Step 1: Select a source dataset and click Select.

Tip: Click on a dataset name to get more information.

Step 2: Limit the dataset by entering keywords, hashtags, and other criteria and then click Preview.>

Tip: For stats on your dataset, see the Dataset statistics tab.

Tip: To view some sample tweets, see the Sample tweets tab.

Step 3: Click Create dataset.

This freezes the dataset parameters. You will receive an email when the dataset files have been created.

Tip: Your dataset now has a URL which you can return to later.

Tip: You can find your datasets under Previous datasets.

Step 4: From the Dataset exports tab, generate and download dataset exports.

Tip: Be patient. These make take a while.
